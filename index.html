What is Industrial Automation and SCADA?

Industrial automation is the control of machinery and processes by autonomous control systems and software. PLC's (Programmable Logic Controllers) could be considered the brain of an automation system. A PLC scans feedback from the field and sends output signals according to the logic or program. An HMI (human machine interface) is the graphic display used by the operator to control and monitor the machinery.

Supervisory control and data acquisition or SCADA is a control system architecture comprising computers, networked data communications and graphical user interfaces for high-level supervision of machines and processes.
